About the Studio

About RC AI Studio

RC AI Studio is an AI-powered creative video studio founded by Naga Ram Charan in Bengaluru, India.

The studio creates modern and visually engaging videos using AI tools, cinematic storytelling and creative editing. Although the journey started recently, RC AI Studio is focused on learning quickly, experimenting with new visual styles and delivering professional content for individuals, creators and growing businesses.

Each project is created with attention to visual quality, storytelling, brand presentation and audience engagement.

The goal is to help clients turn simple ideas into memorable videos that can be used for weddings, advertisements, product launches, social media campaigns and brand promotions.
